ABOUT US

KFC specializes in delicious food featuring fresh ingredients and masterful preparation by the KFC culinary team. Whether you are ordering a multi-course meal or grabbing a drink and pizza at the bar, KFC s lively, casual yet upscale atmosphere makes it perfect for dining with friends, family, clients and business associates. Initially Blue Room was started with the simple idea of serving exceptional fries, burgers and smoothies. It has since evolved into a popular all dining café serving specially selected cuisine from all over the world. KFC maintains a long-standing tradition of quality, offering customers quality food at fair prices. Our products are always prepared with the highest quality ingredients, and are certain to please the most discriminating connoisseurs. For decades people of diverse cultures and backgrounds have gathered together at KFC, and in this spirit our menu comprises of select dishes inspired from delicacies from different cultures around the world. Join us to experience a unique culinary world adventure on your plates at KFC!
